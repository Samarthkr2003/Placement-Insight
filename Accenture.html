Associate Software Engineer
 Round 1: Online Assesement Round-
  • Logical Reasoning
  • English
  • Cloud Computing
  • MS Office
  • Pseudocode
  • Basic aptitude
It consists of 90 MCQ questions and the duration is 90 min. After clearing this round, you will be immediately directed to the Second round which is Coding.
Round 2: Coding round - This round consists of 2 coding questions which were of moderate level and the language is flexible. The time duration is 45 min. After the coding round, the email will be sent to the candidates who have cleared the coding round within an hour for the next Communication assessment round.

Round 3: Communication assessment - After the coding round we will get the Communication assessment link within 4 days. The communication assessment is basically used to test your verbal, listing and speaking skills.
  • Reading
  • Listening
  • Q&A
  • Jumbled sentence
  • Retelling a story
  • Speaking


Round 4: Interview Round- you will receive the email for interview slot booking link, after completing the round 3. These are the questions asked during my interview –
  • Tell me about yourself
  • Tell me about your recent project and which tech stack you have used.
  • why you chose this project.
  • Any feedback you have received from your mentor that you incorporated in the project.
  • Work alone or in a team, challenges faced in a team during project, how you overcome the challenges.
  • More about your extracurricular activities.

Round 2: Coding Round- 45 Minutes. No of Questions: 2 -
Difficulty: Easy-Medium-Hard(it can be any level for me it’s an easy level string based)
